# OG Image Instructions

The SEO implementation is expecting an OG (Open Graph) image at: `public/og-image.jpg`

## Image Specifications:
- **Dimensions**: 1200x630 pixels
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File name**: `og-image.jpg`
- **Location**: `/public/og-image.jpg`

## Design Recommendations:

### Option 1: Create Your Own
- Use Figma, Canva, or Photoshop
- Use a dark background (#000000 to #1a1a1a)
- Add your name "SHOAIB" in large, bold text
- Include subtitle: "Product Designer & UI/UX Expert"  
- Use accent colors: #667eea and #764ba2 (purple/violet gradient)
- Keep it minimal and professional

### Option 2: Use a Template
- Visit [Canva OG Image Templates](https://www.canva.com/templates/s/og-image/)
- Filter for 1200x630 size
- Customize with your brand colors
- Download and save as `og-image.jpg` in the `public` folder

### Option 3: Use a Generator Tool
- [OG Image Generator](https://og-image.vercel.app/)
- [Social Media Graphics](https://www.socialsizes.io/)
- [@vercel/og](https://vercel.com/docs/functions/og-image-generation) - Generate dynamically

## Temporary Solution:
Until you create your OG image, the site will use a fallback. However, creating a custom image will significantly improve social media sharing appearance on:
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- Facebook
- Discord
- Slack
- WhatsApp

## Testing Your OG Image:
After creating and uploading your image, test it at:
